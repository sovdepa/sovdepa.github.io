Conditions Presumed To Be Caused By Military Service Under The PACT Act

This quick reference guide was created by SOVDEPA and was last updated in August 2025. Updates will continue as new presumptive conditions are added by the VA under the PACT Act. These handouts provide next steps for Veterans interested in filing a claim related to the PACT Act or wanting to connect with the VA regarding their military toxic exposure concerns.
