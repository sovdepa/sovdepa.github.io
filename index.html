The Society of Veteran Disability Examiner PAs (SOVDEPA) is a professional medical organization dedicated to advancing high-quality, Veteran-centered care through excellence in disability medical examinations. We strive to expand and support the growing role of PAs in this specialized, evolving field while increasing awareness of conditions uniquey associated with military service.

Recognized by the American Academy of PAs (AAPA) as a Special Interest Group in 2023, SOVDEPA offers educational resources and networking opportunities for PAs at no charge. Amid current and projected shortages of medical disability examiners, SOVDEPA actively advocates for PAs as essential providers in ensuring timely, accurate, and compassionate evaluations for Veterans.

In addition to supporting disability examiners, SOVDEPA is committed to educating all PAs, across every specialty, about health conditions linked to toxic exposures during military service, including those recognized under the PACT Act. Many Veterans receive care in civilian healthcare settings and may not readily disclose their military service, placing them at risk for delayed or missed diagnoses related to service-connected exposures. SOVDEPA promotes the importance of routinely asking all patients about prior military service and understanding how that history may impact current and future health.

By increasing awareness of military-related toxic exposures and service-connected conditions, SOVDEPA aims to empower PAs in every practice setting to provide more informed, comprehensive medical care for Veterans - improving clinical outcomes while honoring their service. To support clinicians who may have limited familiarity with the VA system, SOVDEPA offers Veteran-focused resources on our website to help guide Veterans on how to begin the disability claims process. We also curate educational links to reputable, no-cost resources on military toxic exposures, including access to an exposure-education mobile application that can be used at the point of care.

PAs are invited to engage with SOVDEPA by signing up via email for our quarterly newsletter, following us on LinkedIn, or subscribing to our YouTube channel for free educational videos. These platforms provide ongoing updates, education, and shared insights to support PAs caring for Veterans across all practice settings. SOVDEPA is an entirely volunteer-run organization, and all resources, educational content, and networking opportunties are offered at no cost.

*SOVDEPA is run entirely by volunteers with no budget. SOVDEPA does not accept or distribute funds.
SOVDEPA does not handle job placement, employment matters, or pay questions, and does not respond to solicitations.
