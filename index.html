The Society of Veteran Disability Examiner PAs (SOVDEPA) is a professional medical organization dedicated to providing high-quality care to Veterans receiving disability medical examinations. We strive to expand the number of PAs serving Veterans in this cutting-edge field and raise awareness of unique conditions caused by military service.

The American Academy of PAs (AAPA) recognized SOVDEPA as a special interest group in 2023. SOVDEPA offers educational resources and networking at no charge. With the current and future projected shortages of Veteran medical disability examiners, SOVDEPA will continue to advocate for PAs in this field.

*SOVDEPA is run entirely by volunteers with no budget. SOVDEPA does not accept funds or distribute funds.
SOVDEPA does not handle job placement, employment matters, or pay questions. SOVDEPA does not respond to solicitations of any type.
